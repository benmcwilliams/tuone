Project progress:
- [invest](https://invest.wfbb.de/en/future-mobility/battery-expertise-brandenburg, No date provided): - The battery production projects in Brandenburg are in various stages of development. Rock Tech is developing Europe’s first lithium converter, BASF is producing cathode material on a large scale, SVolt is preparing for comprehensive cell production, and Microvast will manufacture complete modules and packs. Additionally, BASF and Botree are planning to recycle batteries. The Tesla Gigafactory in Grünheide also plans to produce its own batteries, contributing to Brandenburg's aim of achieving a closed loop in battery technology.
- [electrive](https://www.electrive.com/2022/09/10/svolt-confirms-2nd-battery-cell-plant-in-germany/, 2022-09-10): - SVOLT took over the plant in Lauchhammer, including buildings and infrastructure, from the previous owner at the end of August. The company plans to repurpose the majority of the existing halls and is developing a master plan including a detailed schedule for all modernization and building measures. The construction of the Saarland sites is facing delays, with production in Überherrn expected to start no earlier than 2027, four years later than initially announced.
- [batteriesnews](https://batteriesnews.com/svolt-builds-additional-battery-cell-factory-brandenburg-germany-european-market/, 2022-09-12): - SVOLT has taken over the former Vestas site in Lauchhammer, which ceased production of rotor blades. The majority of the existing halls will be repurposed for the battery cell factory, with rebuilding and extension works necessary. SVOLT is currently developing a master plan for the project, including detailed schedules and necessary rebuilding, modernization, and extension measures.
- [electrive](https://www.electrive.com/2023/10/24/svolt-plans-another-battery-plant-outside-china/, 2023-10-24): - The decision-making process, potential start of production, and detailed location in Finland have not been mentioned yet. The conversion of the plant in Lauchhammer, Brandenburg, is currently underway, and the start of construction of the factory in Saarland has been postponed to at least 2027 due to local protests.
- [energytrend](https://www.energytrend.com/news/20220930-29937.html, 2017-06-08): - SVOLT's first factory in Germany, located in Heusweiler, Saarland, is currently being built and will be used to assemble battery packs once fully set up. The second factory in Lauchhammer, Brandenburg, will involve transforming a defunct facility that was previously used to manufacture wind turbines into a battery cell manufacturing site. - CATL's first factory in Germany, located in the state of Thuringia, is in its construction phase and is scheduled to commence operations before the end of the year. The second factory in Hungary, located in the Debrecen Industrial Park, will begin its construction phase this year.
