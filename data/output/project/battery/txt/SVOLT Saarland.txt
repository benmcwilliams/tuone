Project progress:
- [electrive](https://www.electrive.com/2022/09/10/svolt-confirms-2nd-battery-cell-plant-in-germany/, 2022-09-10): - SVOLT is planning to start production in Brandenburg in early 2025. The company aims to repurpose the former wind turbine plant in Lauchhammer, with rebuilding and extension works expected to be necessary. The construction of the Saarland sites is facing delays, with production in Überherrn now expected to start in 2027, four years later than initially announced.
- [electrive](https://www.electrive.com/2022/12/12/svolt-signs-lease-for-battery-factory-in-germany/, 2022-12-12): - The assembly of battery cells into modules and packs at the Heusweiler site is scheduled to begin in April 2023, with the final completion contractually agreed by mid-2024. The cell factory in Überherrn is significantly delayed, with cell production not expected before 2027.
- [energytrend](https://www.energytrend.com/news/20220930-29937.html, 2017-06-08): - SVOLT's first factory in Germany, located in Heusweiler, Saarland, is still being built and will be used to assemble battery packs once fully set up. - SVOLT's second factory in Lauchhammer, Brandenburg, will involve transforming a defunct facility that was formerly used to manufacture wind turbines into a battery cell manufacturing site. - CATL's first factory in Germany, located in the state of Thuringia, is scheduled to commence operation before the end of the year, and its second factory in Hungary will begin its construction phase this year.
