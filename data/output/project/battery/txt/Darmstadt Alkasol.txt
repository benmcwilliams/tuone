Project progress:
- [electrive](https://www.electrive.com/2021/08/17/akasol-starts-up-battery-production-at-new-plant-in-darmstadt/, 2021-08-17): - Akasol had moved into the new headquarters in October 2020 and started production with an initial one GWh. The ceremonial opening of the plant did not take place last autumn due to pandemic-related restrictions. - With the addition of new production lines, a capacity of 2.5 GWh is expected to be reached in 2022, with up to five gigawatt-hours possible depending on customer demand.
- [sustainable-bus](https://www.sustainable-bus.com/components/akasols-new-plant-in-darmstadt-will-have-fully-automated-production-lines-by-manz/, 2020-02-11): - The Gigafactory 1 is expected to be inaugurated by the middle of 2021. - The company has commissioned the production equipment manufacturer Manz AG to supply the first fully automated module production lines for the Gigafactory 1. - The investment focus in the next few months will be on the construction of the new headquarters, including a test and validation center, main administration building, and space for up to 500 workplaces.
- [borgwarner](https://www.borgwarner.com/newsroom/press-releases/2021/02/15/borgwarner-agrees-to-acquire-akasol-ag-seeking-to-expand-electrification-portfolio, 2021-02-15): - The Business Combination Agreement has been signed between BorgWarner and AKASOL, with a voluntary public takeover offer at €120.00 per share in cash for all outstanding shares of AKASOL. The Offer is expected to be completed late in the second quarter of 2021, subject to regulatory approvals and other closing conditions.
- [truckandbusbuilder](https://truckandbusbuilder.com/article/2019/04/01/akasol-announces-further-investment-to-expand-battery-production, 2019-04-01): - Akasol AG has opened a new EUR10m headquarters and production facility in Langen during 2018. They are now making further investment to build a new and larger facility in Darmstadt. The immediate start of construction on a 20,000 square metre site on the former US Kelley Barracks property is planned.
- [insideevs](https://insideevs.com/news/440390/akasol-dynamic-growth-demand-ev-batteries-us/, 2020-08-23): - Construction of the facility in Hazel Park, Michigan began in H1 2020 and is expected to be operational later this year. The expansion in Germany includes an expanded plant in Langen and the construction of a new plant in Darmstadt, with a goal for 2 GWh annually by H2 2021.
- [electrive](https://www.electrive.com/2020/04/21/akasol-orders-battery-production-systems-from-woll/, 2020-04-21): - The new battery systems of the type AKASystem AKM CYC will be manufactured in Darmstadt from mid-2021. - The company plans to expand the Darmstadt production facility and start operating "Gigafactory 2" in North America from early 2022.
