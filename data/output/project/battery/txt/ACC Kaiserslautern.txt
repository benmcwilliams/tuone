Location:
- [electrive](https://www.electrive.com/2021/09/02/franco-german-acc-granted-funding-to-make-batteries-in-germany/, 2021-09-02): - The battery cell factory project is located in Kaiserslautern, Germany, as part of the EU’s Battery IPCEI.
- [insideevs](https://insideevs.com/news/530878/germany-acc-437-million-gigafactory/, 2021-09-06): - Germany, Kaiserslautern, Rhineland-Palatinate state
- [nordfranceinvest](https://www.nordfranceinvest.com/gigafactory-confirmed-for-hauts-de-france/, No date provided): - The project involves the construction of automotive gigafactories in Douvrin, France (Hauts-de-France region) and Kaiserslautern, Germany.

Capacity:
- [electrive](https://www.electrive.com/2021/09/02/franco-german-acc-granted-funding-to-make-batteries-in-germany/, 2021-09-02): - The project aims to achieve a total production capacity of 24 GWh per year, with an initial phase leading to 8 GWh output scheduled to start in 2023. Battery production in Kaiserslautern is expected to begin in 2025.
- [insideevs](https://insideevs.com/news/530878/germany-acc-437-million-gigafactory/, 2021-09-06): - Planned capacity of 8 GWh annually, with expansion to 24 GWh annually later this decade
- [nordfranceinvest](https://www.nordfranceinvest.com/gigafactory-confirmed-for-hauts-de-france/, No date provided): - The cumulative capacity of both sites is planned to reach 48GWh by 2030, with production set to start in 2023.

Investment amounts:
- [electrive](https://www.electrive.com/2021/09/02/franco-german-acc-granted-funding-to-make-batteries-in-germany/, 2021-09-02): - The federal and state governments are funding the project with a total of 436.8 million euros. The companies involved in the joint venture are investing over two billion euros for this purpose.
- [insideevs](https://insideevs.com/news/530878/germany-acc-437-million-gigafactory/, 2021-09-06): - Total funding of about €437 million ($519 million), including €386 million from the Federal Ministry of Economics and Technology and €52 million from the state of Rhineland-Palatinate - Intended investment of €5 billion in the two plants, with €1.3 billion expected to come from the European Union
- [nordfranceinvest](https://www.nordfranceinvest.com/gigafactory-confirmed-for-hauts-de-france/, No date provided): - The project represents an investment of more than €5 billion, including financial support of €1.3 billion from the French and German public authorities.

Project progress:
- [electrive](https://www.electrive.com/2021/09/02/franco-german-acc-granted-funding-to-make-batteries-in-germany/, 2021-09-02): - ACC, the Franco-German consortium, fired the starting gun to kick-off pilot production in January 2020 at a Saft facility in France. The first phase of construction is set to start in 2023, with battery production in Kaiserslautern expected to begin in 2025.
- [insideevs](https://insideevs.com/news/530878/germany-acc-437-million-gigafactory/, 2021-09-06): - Established in September 2020 - Construction scheduled to start in 2023 - Production expected to begin in 2025 - Expected to supply enough battery cells for up to 500,000 electric cars annually
- [nordfranceinvest](https://www.nordfranceinvest.com/gigafactory-confirmed-for-hauts-de-france/, No date provided): - The project was announced in January 2020 and confirmed in September 2020. The R&D and development have already started at the R&D site in Bordeaux and the pilot site in Nersac. Mass production is due to start in 2023.

Project type:
- [electrive](https://www.electrive.com/2021/09/02/franco-german-acc-granted-funding-to-make-batteries-in-germany/, 2021-09-02): - The project involves the construction of a new battery cell factory in Kaiserslautern, Germany.
- [insideevs](https://insideevs.com/news/530878/germany-acc-437-million-gigafactory/, 2021-09-06): - New build gigafactory
- [nordfranceinvest](https://www.nordfranceinvest.com/gigafactory-confirmed-for-hauts-de-france/, No date provided): - The project involves the construction of new gigafactories for battery production.

Value chain stages:
- [electrive](https://www.electrive.com/2021/09/02/franco-german-acc-granted-funding-to-make-batteries-in-germany/, 2021-09-02): - The project covers cell technology development and battery production, aiming to produce battery cells for around 500,000 electric vehicles annually.
- [insideevs](https://insideevs.com/news/530878/germany-acc-437-million-gigafactory/, 2021-09-06): - Cathode and anode manufacturing - Cell fabrication - Module assembly
- [nordfranceinvest](https://www.nordfranceinvest.com/gigafactory-confirmed-for-hauts-de-france/, No date provided): - The project covers R&D, mass production, and aims to equip 1 million electric vehicles per year, representing more than 10% of the European market.
