Project progress:
- [magnastorage](https://www.magnastorage.cz/single-post/2020/06/23/The-grand-opening-of-the-MES-HE3DA-battery-factory-will-take-place-in-September, 2020-06-23): The Magna Energy Storage (MES) manufacturing plant for the HE3DA batteries is set to be officially opened on Thursday, September 17, 2020. The production technologies are being finalized, and the batteries are undergoing a series of tests. The plant is described as one of the first to be completely robotized and automated, meeting the requirements for Industry 4.0 qualification.
- [apps](https://apps.eurofound.europa.eu/restructuring-events/detail/101817, No date provided): - The project officially opened a new plant for the production of high-capacity batteries in September 2020 and plans to start commercial production at the beginning of 2021. The employment effect is expected to start from January 2021 and continue until December 2025.
- [magnastorage](https://www.magnastorage.cz/single-post/new-thin-walled-battery-cell-manufactured-on-the-serial-production-line-in-horn%C3%AD-such%C3%A1, No date provided): - The battery is currently being produced on a smaller serial production line, with plans to start production on a large serial production line for mass production by the end of 2022.

The factory is planned to be fully operational and highly automated, with a focus on exporting batteries. The workforce is projected to grow from 250 initially to potentially hundreds more after the production expansion.
